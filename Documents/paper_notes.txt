NMR:
* closer to native state
* protein in solution
* lower resolution

X-Ray:
* better resolution
* needs to crystalize well
* crystal-packing interactions
* no hydrogens positions

Databases of "good" NMR protein structures:
* DRESS: a Database of REfined Solution NMR Structures
http://www.cmbi.ru.nl/dress/
* Critical Assessment of Automated Structure Determination of Proteins from NMR Data
http://www.wenmr.eu/wenmr/casd-nmr-data-sets
